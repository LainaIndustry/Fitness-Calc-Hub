Heart Rate Zones Explained

Zone % of Max HR Benefits Feeling
Very Light 50-60% Recovery, basic health Easy, comfortable
Light 60-70% Fat burning, endurance Conversational pace
Moderate 70-80% Aerobic fitness Breathing deeper
Hard 80-90% Anaerobic capacity Breathing hard
Maximum 90-100% Peak performance Maximum effort

How to Use This Information

  • Weight Loss: Aim for 60-70% of max HR for optimal fat burning
  • Cardiovascular Fitness: Train at 70-85% of max HR
  • Recovery Days: Stay at 50-60% of max HR
  • Interval Training: Alternate between 80-90% and 50-60%

Note: These are estimates. Individual variations may occur. Consult a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program.
