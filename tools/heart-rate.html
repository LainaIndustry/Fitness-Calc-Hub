Understanding Heart Rate Zones

Heart rate zones help you train at the right intensity for your fitness goals. Each zone corresponds to a different percentage of your maximum heart rate.

Heart Rate Zones:

  • Zone 1 (50-60%): Very light intensity, good for warm-up, recovery, and beginners
  • Zone 2 (60-70%): Light intensity, improves basic endurance and fat burning
  • Zone 3 (70-80%): Moderate intensity, improves aerobic capacity
  • Zone 4 (80-90%): Hard intensity, improves anaerobic capacity and threshold
  • Zone 5 (90-100%): Maximum intensity, for interval training and peak performance

Training Benefits by Zone:

  • Zone 1-2: Builds aerobic base, promotes fat burning
  • Zone 3: Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Zone 4: Increases lactate threshold
  • Zone 5: Develops maximum performance and speed

Note: These calculations use the common formula (220 - age) for maximum heart rate, which is an estimate. Individual maximum heart rates can vary.
