Understanding Heart Rate Zones

Heart rate zones help you train at the right intensity for your fitness goals. Different zones provide different benefits.

Heart Rate Zones:

  • Zone 1 (50-60%): Very light - Warm-up, recovery, fat burning
  • Zone 2 (60-70%): Light - Basic endurance, improves metabolism
  • Zone 3 (70-80%): Moderate - Aerobic fitness, efficient cardio
  • Zone 4 (80-90%): Hard - Anaerobic threshold, performance
  • Zone 5 (90-100%): Maximum - Peak performance, short bursts

Your maximum heart rate is estimated as 220 minus your age. For more accurate results, consider getting a professional assessment.
