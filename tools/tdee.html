Understanding TDEE

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories you burn each day, including all activities and exercise.

TDEE Components:

  • BMR (60-75%): Calories burned at complete rest
  • Thermic Effect of Food (10%): Calories burned digesting food
  • Physical Activity (15-30%): Calories burned through exercise and daily activities

Using Your TDEE:

  • Weight Maintenance: Eat your TDEE in calories
  • Weight Loss: Eat 300-500 calories less than your TDEE
  • Weight Gain: Eat 300-500 calories more than your TDEE
