What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories you burn in a day. It's calculated by multiplying your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) by your activity level.

Note: These are estimates. Individual results may vary based on metabolism, muscle mass, and other factors.
