About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Categories:

Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obesity

Note: BMI is a screening tool and does not directly measure body fat. It may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or the elderly.
