What is BMR?

Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production while at complete rest.

How to Use Your BMR

  • Weight Maintenance: Multiply your BMR by your activity level to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)
  • Weight Loss: Create a calorie deficit below your TDEE
  • Weight Gain: Create a calorie surplus above your TDEE

Note: BMR is an estimate. Individual variations may occur based on muscle mass, genetics, and other factors.
