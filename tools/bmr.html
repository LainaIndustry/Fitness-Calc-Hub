About BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production.

What affects BMR?

  • Age: BMR decreases with age
  • Gender: Men typically have higher BMR
  • Weight: Heavier individuals have higher BMR
  • Height: Taller people have higher BMR
  • Body Composition: Muscle burns more calories than fat

Note: This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is considered the most accurate BMR formula.
