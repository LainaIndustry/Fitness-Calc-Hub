Understanding BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to accomplish its most basic (basal) life-sustaining functions at complete rest.

What BMR Includes:

  • Breathing and circulation
  • Cell production
  • Nutrient processing
  • Protein synthesis
  • Maintaining body temperature

Your BMR accounts for about 60-75% of the total calories you burn each day. Factors that affect BMR include age, gender, weight, height, body composition, and genetics.
