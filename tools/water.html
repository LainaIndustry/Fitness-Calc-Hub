Importance of Proper Hydration

Water is essential for nearly every bodily function. Proper hydration supports digestion, circulation, temperature regulation, and nutrient transport.

Benefits of Proper Hydration:

  • Improved physical performance and endurance
  • Better cognitive function and mood
  • Enhanced digestion and nutrient absorption
  • Healthy skin and detoxification
  • Optimal joint and muscle function

Signs of Dehydration:

  • Thirst and dry mouth
  • Fatigue and dizziness
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Headaches
  • Reduced exercise performance

Note: Individual water needs may vary based on health conditions, medications, and other factors. Listen to your body's thirst signals.
