Understanding Your Water Needs

Your daily water requirement is calculated based on:

  • Base Requirement: 30-35ml per kg of body weight
  • Activity Level: Increased needs for exercise and sweat loss
  • Climate: Additional needs in hot, humid, or dry conditions

Hydration Tips

  • Drink water consistently throughout the day
  • Increase intake before, during, and after exercise
  • Monitor urine color - pale yellow indicates good hydration
  • Eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables
  • Listen to your body's thirst signals

Signs of Dehydration

  • Thirst and dry mouth
  • Fatigue or dizziness
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Headaches
  • Dry skin

Note: Individual water needs can vary based on health conditions, medications, and other factors. This is a general guideline.
