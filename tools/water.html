Importance of Hydration

Water is essential for nearly every function in your body. Proper hydration helps with:

  • Regulating body temperature
  • Transporting nutrients and oxygen
  • Lubricating joints
  • Flushing out waste products
  • Supporting cognitive function
  • Maintaining healthy skin

Signs of Dehydration:

  • Thirst
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Fatigue or dizziness
  • Dry mouth and skin
  • Headaches

Note: Individual water needs can vary based on climate, health conditions, and other factors. Listen to your body and drink when you're thirsty.
