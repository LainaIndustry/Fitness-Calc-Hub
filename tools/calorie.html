Understanding Calorie Needs

Calories are units of energy that your body uses to function. The right calorie intake depends on your goals, activity level, and metabolism.

Calorie Guidelines:

  • Weight Loss: 300-500 calories less than your TDEE
  • Weight Maintenance: Equal to your TDEE
  • Weight Gain: 300-500 calories more than your TDEE

Note: For sustainable results, aim for gradual weight changes of 0.5-1 kg (1-2 lbs) per week.
