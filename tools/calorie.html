Understanding Your Calorie Needs

Your daily calorie target is based on your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) adjusted for your specific weight goal:

  • Extreme Weight Loss: 40% deficit from TDEE
  • Weight Loss: 20% deficit from TDEE
  • Mild Weight Loss: 10% deficit from TDEE
  • Weight Maintenance: Equal to your TDEE
  • Weight Gain: 10% surplus above TDEE

Tips for Success

  • Track your food intake using a food diary or app
  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods rather than just calories
  • Be consistent but allow for flexibility
  • Adjust your intake based on progress and how you feel

Note: These are estimates. Individual needs may vary. Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
