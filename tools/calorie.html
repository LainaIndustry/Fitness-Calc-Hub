About Calorie Calculation

Calorie needs vary based on your goals. Use this calculator to determine your optimal daily calorie intake.

Weight Loss Tips:

  • Aim for a 500-calorie deficit for safe weight loss (0.5kg/week)
  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods
  • Combine diet with regular exercise
  • Don't go below 1200 calories (women) or 1500 calories (men)

Weight Gain Tips:

  • Aim for a 250-500 calorie surplus
  • Focus on protein-rich foods
  • Combine with strength training
  • Choose healthy calorie sources

Note: These are estimates. Individual needs may vary based on metabolism and other factors.
