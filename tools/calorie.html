Understanding Your Calorie Needs

Your daily calorie needs are calculated based on your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and your activity level, then adjusted based on your fitness goals.

Activity Level Definitions:

  • Sedentary: Little or no exercise
  • Light: Light exercise 1-3 times per week
  • Moderate: Moderate exercise 3-5 times per week
  • Active: Hard exercise 6-7 times per week
  • Very Active: Very hard exercise, physical job, or training twice a day

Remember: These calculations provide estimates. Individual calorie needs can vary based on metabolism, body composition, and other factors.
