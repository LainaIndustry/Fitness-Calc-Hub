Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

FitCalc is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information through our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

2. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Your fitness calculations remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies to monitor your activity on our website.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts and Font Awesome for typography and icons. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
